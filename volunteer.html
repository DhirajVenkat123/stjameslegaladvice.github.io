Volunteering & Vacancies

Join our team and help widen access to justice in North London.

Professional legal advisers needed

St James Legal Advice Centre (SJLAC) has been providing free legal advice in Haringey (Muswell Hill, Colney Hatch and Wood Green) for over twenty years. During that time our clinics have helped thousands of people with their justice problems – housing, consumer rights, benefits claims, family issues and employment rights. Legal aid is now rarely available for these types of issues, so SJLAC’s work is vital in helping to address this “justice gap”.

We are always looking for more legal advisers for our Tuesday morning, Wednesday afternoon and Saturday morning clinics. We’re looking for legal professionals (barristers or solicitors, possibly retired or on a career break) who could give half a day a fortnight or once a month to provide advice.

No prior experience in the areas of law we address is necessary or expected. Current advisers had no experience in these areas before they started volunteering. We provide training, support and oversight to ensure that volunteer legal advisers are confident before they start giving advice on their own. If you do have expertise in relevant areas of law that would be very welcome. You’ll be part of a great team, including a wonderful group of law students from Middlesex University and other training organisations who provide reception, triage and research backup at the clinics.

If you have legal expertise and some time, please consider volunteering with us. Contact Ann Jenkins at manager@stjameslegaladvice.org to arrange a taster session.

Volunteer Legal Assistant – Pro Bono Opportunity

Calling all law graduates & students: make a real impact with your legal skills! Are you a passionate law student or recent graduate eager to gain hands-on experience and make a difference? Join our team dedicated to providing access to justice for those who need it most and support our work in Muswell Hill and Wood Green.

We’re looking for motivated, socially minded legal volunteers to support our work across a range of meaningful cases – from housing and immigration to employment and family law. This is your chance to gain valuable experience while helping people who need legal support the most.

What we’re looking for

  • Law students (LPC, SQE or undergraduate) or recent graduates
  • Strong communication and organisational skills
  • A genuine interest in social justice and helping vulnerable communities
  • Availability to volunteer weekly across the academic year (flexible)

Why volunteer with us?

  • ✔ Real legal experience
  • ✔ Mentorship from experienced solicitors
  • ✔ Work that actually changes lives
  • ✔ Friendly, inclusive team culture

Ready to stand up for justice? Send your CV and a short cover email to manager@stjameslegaladvice.org with the subject line: “Volunteer Legal Assistant Application”.
