Our Clinics

Saturday Clinic at St James’s Church, Muswell Hill

Our Saturday morning clinic takes place between 9.30am and 12 noon at St James’s Church, St James’s Lane, Muswell Hill, London N10 3DB. The clinic mainly provides general legal advice through a team of trained student volunteers supervised by legal professionals.

St James’s Church is close to Sainsbury’s and the Everyman cinema. The nearest underground station is Highgate. Nearby bus routes include: 43, 134, 102, 234, W7, and 144.

Open Dates:

  • Autumn Term: 6 September – 20 December 2025
  • Spring Term: 3 January – 28 March 2026
  • Summer Term: 11 April – 25 July 2026

Please note: This clinic operates strictly as a drop-in service. While we can help draft example letters and provide legal guidance, we are unable to take on ongoing cases due to the availability of volunteers.

Tuesday Clinic at Wilton Community Church

Our Tuesday morning clinic runs from 10am to 12 noon at Wilton Community Church, Wilton Road, Muswell Hill, London N10 1LT. In addition to legal advice, volunteers can help with form-filling, reviewing documents, and drafting simple letters or emails.

The church is on the corner of Wilton Road and Coppetts Road. It’s accessible via the 134 bus from Muswell Hill, the 43 to Wilton Road / Colney Hatch Lane, or the 234 along Coppetts Road.

Email: wilton@stjameslegaladvice.org

Open Dates:

  • Autumn Term: 2 September – 16 December 2025
  • Spring Term: 6 January – 17 March 2026
  • Summer Term: 14 April – 28 July 2026

Wednesday Clinic at The Salvation Army Church and Community Centre, Wood Green

Our Wednesday clinic is held between 1pm and 3pm at The Salvation Army Centre, 24 Lymington Avenue, Wood Green, N22 6JA. We offer general legal advice on a drop-in basis, and volunteers can help with completing forms, organising documents, and drafting letters.

The centre is a short walk from Wood Green underground station. Buses serving the area include: 29, 67, 141, 230, and W4.

Email: woodgreen@stjameslegaladvice.org

Open Dates: The clinic is currently closed until September.

  • Autumn Term: 3 September – 10 December 2025

Specialist Clinics

We occasionally run specialist clinics in family law, employment law, and immigration support. These are usually held on specific days at either the Wilton or Wood Green clinics.

Family Law: Held at the Wood Green clinic, usually on the last Wednesday of the month. Upcoming dates for the Summer Term include: 30 April, 4 June, 25 June, and 16 July 2025.

Employment Law:

  • Wilton Clinic: 6 & 27 May, 17 June, 1 & 15 July 2025
  • Muswell Hill Clinic: Dates to be confirmed

Immigration Support: Held at Wood Green, generally on the first Wednesday of the month. Upcoming dates for the Summer Term include: 14 May, 4 June, and 2 July 2025.

Please note: Our immigration support clinic can help you understand immigration forms and processes and provide general information. We cannot take on your case, complete forms for you, or offer personalised immigration advice. However, we will refer you to appropriate organisations who can offer that support.
