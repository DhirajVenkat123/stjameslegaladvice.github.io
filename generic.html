Our Clinics

Saturday Clinic at St James’s Church, Muswell Hill

Our Saturday morning clinic runs from 9:30am to 12 noon at St James’s Church, St James’s Lane, Muswell Hill, London N10 3DB. This clinic primarily offers general legal advice and is staffed by trained student volunteers under supervision.

St James’s Church is located near Sainsbury’s and the Everyman cinema. The nearest underground station is Highgate. Buses to Muswell Hill include the 43, 134, 102, 234, W7, and 144.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 6 September – 20 December 2025
Spring Term: 3 January – 28 March 2026
Summer Term: 11 April – 25 July 2026

Please note that our Saturday clinic is strictly drop-in only. All advisers are full-time professionals volunteering their time, so we are unable to take on ongoing cases. We provide one-off advice and may offer example letters to guide you, which you can adapt and use.

Tuesday Clinic at Wilton Community Church

The Tuesday clinic operates from 10am to 12 noon at Wilton Community Church, Wilton Road, Muswell Hill, London N10 1LT. You can contact this clinic at: wilton@stjameslegaladvice.org.

The church is on the corner of Wilton Road and Coppetts Road. It’s accessible via the 134 bus from Muswell Hill, the 43 to Wilton Road/Colney Hatch Lane, or the 234 to Barnet along Coppetts Road.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 2 September – 16 December 2025
Spring Term: 6 January – 17 March 2026
Summer Term: 14 April – 28 July 2026
Note: The clinic will close after the 29 July 2026 session for the summer break.

Wednesday Clinic at The Salvation Army Church and Community Centre, Wood Green

This clinic runs on Wednesdays between 1pm and 3pm at The Salvation Army Centre, 24 Lymington Avenue, Wood Green, London N22 6JA.
Contact: woodgreen@stjameslegaladvice.org

The nearest underground station is Wood Green. Buses serving this area include the 29, 67, 141, 230 and W4.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 3 September – 10 December 2025
The clinic is currently closed until September.

Specialist Clinics:
We also offer specialist sessions in family, employment, and immigration law.

  • Family Law: Usually held on the last Wednesday of each month. Summer term dates: 30 April, 4 June, 25 June, 16 July.
  • Employment Law: Spring term dates at Wilton clinic: 6 & 27 May, 17 June, 1 & 15 July 2025.
  • Immigration Support: Generally held on the first Wednesday of each month. Summer dates: 14 May, 4 June, 2 July 2025.
    Please note: Our immigration support clinic provides general information and helps you understand forms and documents. However, we cannot take on your case, fill in forms for you, or give personalised legal advice. We can signpost you to trusted specialist services if you need further help.
