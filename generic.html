Our Clinics

Saturday Clinic at St James’s Church, Muswell Hill

Our Saturday clinic runs from 9:30am to 12 noon at St James’s Church, St James’s Lane, Muswell Hill, London N10 3DB. It is near Sainsbury’s and the Everyman cinema. The nearest tube is Highgate, and buses include 43, 134, 102, 234, W7, and 144.

This clinic offers general legal advice and is staffed by trained student volunteers under supervision. It is a drop-in clinic only — we cannot take on ongoing cases, but we can offer legal guidance and write example letters to take away.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 6 September – 20 December 2025
Spring Term: 3 January – 28 March 2026
Summer Term: 11 April – 25 July 2026

Tuesday Clinic at Wilton Community Church

This clinic runs from 10am to 12 noon at Wilton Community Church, Wilton Road, Muswell Hill, London N10 1LT.
Email: wilton@stjameslegaladvice.org

Wilton Church is on the corner of Wilton Road and Coppetts Road. You can get there using the 134 bus from Muswell Hill, the 43 to Wilton Road / Colney Hatch Lane, or the 234 to Barnet via Coppetts Road.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 2 September – 16 December 2025
Spring Term: 6 January – 17 March 2026
Summer Term: 14 April – 28 July 2026
The clinic will close after the 29 July 2026 session for the summer.

Wednesday Clinic at The Salvation Army Church and Community Centre, Wood Green

This clinic runs from 1pm to 3pm at The Salvation Army Centre, 24 Lymington Avenue, Wood Green, London N22 6JA.
Email: woodgreen@stjameslegaladvice.org

The nearest tube is Wood Green. Buses include the 29, 67, 141, 230, and W4.

Open Dates:
Autumn Term: 3 September – 10 December 2025
This clinic is currently closed until September.

Specialist Clinics:

  • Family Law Clinic – Usually the last Wednesday of the month at Wood Green.
    Summer Term Dates: 30 April, 4 June, 25 June, 16 July 2025
  • Employment Law Clinics
    Wilton Clinic: 6 & 27 May, 17 June, 1 & 15 July 2025
  • Immigration Support Clinic – Usually the first Wednesday of the month at Wood Green.
    Summer Term Dates: 14 May, 4 June, 2 July 2025

The immigration support clinic can help you understand forms and documents and signpost you to suitable organisations. We cannot give personalised legal advice, take on your case, or complete forms for you — but we will help you know where to go next.
