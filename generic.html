Our Clinics

Saturday Clinic at St James’s Church, Muswell Hill

Our Saturday morning clinic takes place at St James’s Church in Muswell Hill. This clinic primarily offers general legal advice and is run by trained student volunteers under supervision. Capacity is limited, so please arrive early.

Tuesday Clinic at Wilton Community Church

This clinic takes place at Wilton Community Church on Tuesday evenings. In addition to legal advice, volunteers may assist with form-filling, drafting simple letters or emails, and reviewing documents.

Wednesday Clinic at The Salvation Army Church and Community Centre, Wood Green

Our Wednesday evening clinic runs at The Salvation Army Church in Wood Green. It offers general advice on a drop-in basis and can support clients with completing forms, organising documents, and writing letters. Arrive early as the clinic can fill up quickly.
