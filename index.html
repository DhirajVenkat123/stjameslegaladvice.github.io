St James Legal Advice Centre

We are a charity providing free legal advice to those who cannot afford access to justice, empowering clients to understand their legal rights. We are made up of a dedicated team of volunteer solicitors, barristers, trainees and law students.

How we operate:

We provide drop-in services across our three clinics. Appointments cannot be made, and we do not respond to emails requesting advice.

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis. You are more likely to be seen if you arrive early! We close our doors to clients 30 minutes before the clinic closing time. However, if the clinic reaches full capacity earlier, we reluctantly have to close the doors when this happens.

We strive to accommodate everyone who arrives on time. However, if the clinic is too busy, you may be asked to return the next week or visit one of our other locations.

Each advice session is typically limited to 15 minutes, and we can address only one legal issue per visit. If you have multiple issues, you may need to visit more than once; for instance, returning the following week to discuss additional matters.

Clinic Locations

Our drop-in legal advice clinics run weekly at community centres and churches in Muswell Hill and Wood Green. Visit our Clinics page for locations, dates, and transport information.
