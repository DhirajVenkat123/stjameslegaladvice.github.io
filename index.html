Free Legal Advice

What We Do

We provide free, confidential legal advice to those in need across North London. Our trained volunteers and legal professionals help with a range of legal issues including housing, employment, and family matters.

Clinic Locations

Our drop-in legal advice clinics run weekly at community centres and churches in Muswell Hill and Wood Green. Visit our Clinics page for locations, dates, and transport information.
